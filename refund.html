Refund Policy

If, after your purchase of our TN ABC Online Course, you wish to have your payment refunded, we will do so according to the following terms:

No Fault of Tennessee ABC Cards: The price for your TN ABC Online Course includes a $1.50 credit card/debit card/payment processing fee. Tennessee ABC Cards, LLC pays that fee to a payment processing service. If your reason(s) for requesting a refund are not due to any defect or fault on the part of Tennessee ABC Cards, LLC, a $5.00 service fee plus the $1.50 credit card/debit card/payment processing fee will not be refunded, and your refund will consist of the price you paid for the ABC Online Class, less $6.50.

No Credit for Processing Fee: If you request and receive a refund, but then decide that you wish to re enroll in the TN ABC Online Course, no credit shall be given for the $1.50 processing fee withheld in your initial refund.

Time for Refund:

(1) Once a refund has been issued, it takes 3-10 days for the refund to appear back on your credit card/bank account. There is nothing we can do to speed this process along.

(2) If three (3) months have passed since purchasing your TN ABC Online Course, we will not issue a refund for the class.

(3) Once one (1) calendar year has passed after purchasing your TN ABC Online Course, if you have not registered for a class, you will no longer have access to the TN ABC Cards Online Course and will have to repurchase the class to take it.


No Access: If you request and receive a refund, you shall have no access to the TN ABC Online Course from there on. If you access the TN ABC Online Course after requesting a refund, no refund will be given, and all refunds pending will be cancelled. TN ABC Cards, LLC reserves all rights in law and equity.
